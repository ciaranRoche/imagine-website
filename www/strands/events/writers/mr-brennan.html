Francis Brennan


Sunday 21 October 6pm
Large Room - City Hall

Originally hailing from Dublin, Francis Brennan is the co-owner of the five-star Park Hotel Kenmare, in Co. Kerry. Together with his brother John he showed hospitality businesses how to thrive as part of the RTÉ series ‘At Your Service’, and more recently he has graced our screens on RTÉ’s ‘Francis Brennan’s Grand Tour’. He is the bestselling author of It’s the Little Things, Counting My Blessings and will release his new book Francis Brennan’s Book of Household Management with Gill Books in September 2017.
